Central Washington Vegetable Garden Planner

Name: Central Washington Vegetable Garden Planner

This represents vegetable gardeners of an area of the Pacific Northwest.

Purpose

This website will target gardeners living in Central Washington. It will assist vegetable gardeners in determining what plants to plant in spring versus summer. Users will be able to select from an assortment of common home-grown vegetables. Based on their selections, the website will display the estimated best planting times, including whether seeds should be started indoors or planted outdoors as well as local frost dates.

Scenarios

When is the best time to plant vegetables in Central Washington? Usually the best time to plant vegetables in Central Washington is after the last frost date, which is often in late spring. Frost dates are important for gardeners to understand to keep plants growing healthy and thriving. Some plants when exposed to frost can become damaged or even die.

Can I start frost sensitive plants indoors to extend my growing season? Yes, starting frost-sensitive plants indoors can allow gardeners to extend the growing season. By starting seeds indoors, gardeners can give plants a head start before transplanting them outdoors after the last frost date. This can lead to earlier harvests and a longer growing season for certain crops.
